Budget Planner

by ArtivicoLab

A personal budget that lives in your own Google Sheet.

Budget Planner is a phone-first web app that helps you plan your money in one place. It opens in any browser on your phone, tablet, or computer, works offline, and can sync your data to a spreadsheet in your own Google Drive so you always own a backup.

Open Budget Planner

What you can do

  • Build a monthly or per-paycheck budget with income, bills, expenses, savings, and debt
  • Log transactions across accounts, track daily balances, and reconcile against your bank
  • Set up recurring bills and paychecks, and see everything on a money calendar
  • Plan sinking funds and savings goals, and run a snowball or avalanche debt payoff
  • Track net worth, split spending by person, and see the 50/30/20 breakdown of your money

How Budget Planner uses your Google account

Connecting Google is optional. When you choose to connect, Budget Planner requests only the Google Drive "drive.file" permission. This limits access to the single spreadsheet the app creates for you, and nothing else in your Drive.

We use that access for one purpose: to save and sync your budget to that spreadsheet so it is backed up and available across your devices. Your sign-in stays in your browser and talks to Google directly from your device. ArtivicoLab operates no server and never receives, stores, or sells your data. See our Privacy Policy for the full details.

Your data stays yours

Everything works offline on your device first. If you connect Google, your data lives in a spreadsheet in your own Google Drive that you can open, export, or delete any time. There is no subscription, and connecting to Google Sheets is free.
